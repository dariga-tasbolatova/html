About Me

Introducing myself I am going to tell you that I am a professional animator. It would be easy to point at the myriad of achievements and awards that I have accomplished and received during my lifetime. I have created over 300 films over a seven decade career in animation. My films were nominated for 9 Academy Awards. I was presented with a Lifetime Achievement Oscar by the Academy in 1996 and have created or co-created possibly the most memorable and enduring setof characters in history, including Bugs Bunny, Daffy Duck, Marvin Martian, Road Runner & Wile E. Coyote, Pepe le Pew, Michigan J. Frog, Ralph & Sam, Hubie & Bertie, Marc Anthony & Kitty, and dozens more.

Me and Walt Disney are the only two animation professionals to be Director’s Guild of America Honorary Lifetime Member Recipients. I have received four honorary doctorates and was presented with the Chevalier des Arts et Lettres Award from the French Minister of Culture. Was received the Smithsonian Institution 150th Anniversary Medal of Achievement.
